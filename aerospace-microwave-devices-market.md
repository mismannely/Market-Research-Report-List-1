<p><p>The aerospace microwave devices market includes various frequency bands, notably Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) and Extremely High Frequency (EHF). UHF operates in the 300 MHz to 3 GHz range, facilitating communication and radar systems in avionics and satellite communication. In contrast, EHF ranges from 30 GHz to 300 GHz, offering high data rate transmission and enhanced anti-jamming capabilities, making it ideal for advanced military applications and satellite systems. Both frequencies play crucial roles in enhancing aerospace communications and operational efficiency.</p></p>
