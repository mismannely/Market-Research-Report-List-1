<p><p>The animal gelatin capsule market is categorized mainly into three types: bovine, porcine, and piscine. Bovine gelatin, derived from cattle, is widely used due to its strong gelling properties and suitability for various pharmaceutical and dietary applications. Porcine gelatin, sourced from pigs, is favored for its high solubility and better dissolution rates, making it a common choice in the food and supplement industry. Piscine gelatin, obtained from fish, offers a halal and kosher alternative, appealing to consumers with dietary restrictions.</p></p>
