<p><p>The automotive cabin air filter market is primarily divided into two types: particle automotive cabin air filters and charcoal automotive cabin air filters. Particle filters effectively capture dust, pollen, and other airborne particulates, enhancing air quality within the vehicle. In contrast, charcoal filters incorporate activated carbon, which helps eliminate odors and harmful gases, providing an additional layer of protection against pollutants. Both types contribute significantly to passenger comfort and health by ensuring a cleaner and fresher cabin environment.</p></p>
