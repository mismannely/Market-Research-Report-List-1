<p><p>The automotive SoC (System on Chip) market is segmented into three main types: digital, analog, and mixed signal. Digital SoCs handle processing tasks like advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and infotainment, utilizing high-performance computing capabilities. Analog SoCs manage real-world signals, essential for functions like sensor interfacing and power management. Mixed signal SoCs combine both digital and analog functionalities, enabling complex applications by offering efficient signal processing and control. This diversity supports the growing demand for smart and connected vehicles.</p></p>
