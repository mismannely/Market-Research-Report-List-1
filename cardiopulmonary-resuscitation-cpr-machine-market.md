<p><ul><li>Mechanical CPR Machine</li><li>Automated CPR Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machine market comprises two main types: mechanical CPR machines and automated CPR machines. Mechanical CPR machines provide consistent, high-quality chest compressions by utilizing a mechanism that applies rhythmic pressure to the chest. In contrast, automated CPR machines feature advanced technology that not only delivers compressions but also monitors patient conditions, adjusts compression depth and rate, and provides real-time feedback. Both types enhance the effectiveness of resuscitation efforts during cardiac emergencies, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
