<p><p>Carrier aggregation solutions enhance mobile network performance by combining multiple frequency bands. The market includes various types of cells: </p><p>1. **Picocells** are small, low-power base stations designed for indoor use, supporting a limited number of users.</p><p>2. **Metrocells** provide coverage in densely populated urban areas, bridging the gap between macro and smaller cells.</p><p>3. **Microcells** are slightly larger than picocells, serving specific outdoor areas with moderate traffic.</p><p>4. **Femtocells** target individual homes or small businesses, optimizing coverage and capacity in localized environments.</p></p>
