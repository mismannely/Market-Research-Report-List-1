<p><p>Chain hotel property management systems (PMS) can be categorized into cloud-based and local-based markets. Cloud-based PMS offer centralized management via the internet, enabling real-time access, scalability, and lower upfront costs. This model is ideal for chains with multiple locations, as it facilitates seamless integration and updates. Conversely, local-based PMS are installed on-site, providing full control over data security and customization, often preferred by hotels prioritizing privacy and localized management solutions. Both types cater to varying operational needs and infrastructure preferences.</p></p>
