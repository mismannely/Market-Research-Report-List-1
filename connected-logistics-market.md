<p><p>Connected Logistics encompasses three key market types: Device Management, Application Management, and Connectivity Management. Device Management focuses on overseeing and optimizing the performance of connected devices, ensuring they function efficiently in the logistics chain. Application Management involves developing and maintaining software solutions that facilitate logistics operations, enhancing data analysis and workflow. Connectivity Management refers to the integration and monitoring of communication networks that link devices and applications, ensuring seamless data flow and real-time visibility across the entire logistics ecosystem.</p></p>
