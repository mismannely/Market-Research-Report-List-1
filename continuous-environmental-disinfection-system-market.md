<p><p>The Continuous Environmental Disinfection System market is categorized into Single Mode and Dual Mode based systems. Single Mode systems typically utilize one disinfecting mechanism, such as ultraviolet (UV) light or chemical sprays, to continuously eliminate pathogens in a given environment. In contrast, Dual Mode systems combine two distinct disinfection methods, enhancing overall effectiveness by leveraging the strengths of each technique. This dual approach can provide a broader spectrum of microbial control and improve safety in various settings, such as healthcare and public spaces.</p></p>
