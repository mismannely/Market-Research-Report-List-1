<p><strong>The Diabetes Drug Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral</li><li>Subcutaneous</li><li>Intravenous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diabetes drug therapy encompasses various administration methods, including oral, subcutaneous, and intravenous options. Oral medications, such as metformin and sulfonylureas, are commonly used for type 2 diabetes management, offering convenience and ease of use. Subcutaneous therapies, including insulin injections and GLP-1 receptor agonists, are vital for both type 1 and advanced type 2 diabetes, allowing for controlled glucose levels. Intravenous drug delivery, though less common, is primarily utilized in hospital settings for immediate and precise treatment of severe hyperglycemia or diabetic emergencies.</p></p>
