<p><p>The disposable pipette tips market is primarily divided into two types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general laboratory use, allowing precise liquid handling without additional filtration, making them suitable for routine applications. In contrast, filtered pipette tips incorporate a barrier that prevents aerosol contamination and protects the pipette from liquid spills, making them ideal for sensitive assays and hazardous materials. Both types cater to specific laboratory needs, enhancing accuracy and safety in liquid transfer tasks.</p></p>
