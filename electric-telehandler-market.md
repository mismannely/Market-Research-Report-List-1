<p><p>The electric telehandler market is primarily divided into two types: all-electric and hybrid models. All-electric telehandlers operate solely on electric power, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs, making them ideal for indoor and environmentally sensitive applications. In contrast, hybrid telehandlers combine electric and traditional fuel sources, providing enhanced flexibility and extended operating range. This makes hybrids suitable for various tasks, particularly in outdoor environments where longer runtimes and power versatility are essential. Both types aim to reduce environmental impact while maintaining operational efficiency.</p></p>
