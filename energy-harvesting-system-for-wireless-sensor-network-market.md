<p><p>Energy harvesting systems for wireless sensor networks utilize various methods to capture and convert ambient energy into electrical energy, extending the lifespan of sensor nodes. Light energy harvesting harnesses solar or ambient light through photovoltaic cells. Vibration energy harvesting converts mechanical energy from vibrations into electrical energy using piezoelectric materials. Thermal energy harvesting captures temperature differentials to generate power, often through thermoelectric generators. Other methods may include electromagnetic or radio frequency energy harvesting, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
