<p><p>Enterprise Legal Management Software (ELMS) can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ELMS offers remote access, scalability, and automatic updates, making it ideal for organizations seeking flexibility and lower upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises ELMS is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, which is often preferred by large enterprises with stringent compliance requirements. Both types aim to streamline legal processes, improve efficiency, and enhance collaboration.</p></p>
