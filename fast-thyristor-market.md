<p><p>The Fast Thyristor market comprises two primary types: capsule type and stud type. Capsule type thyristors, typically encapsulated in a small housing, are designed for applications requiring compactness and efficiency, making them ideal for high-density circuits. In contrast, stud type thyristors feature a larger design with a threaded mounting, allowing for better heat dissipation and higher current handling, suitable for industrial and power electronic applications. Both types cater to varying requirements in power control and switching technologies.</p></p>
