<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensing market is divided into two main types: Distributed Sensing and Fiber Optic Point Sensing. Distributed Sensing utilizes optical fibers to measure physical changes over extended lengths, enabling real-time monitoring of large structures like bridges and pipelines. In contrast, Fiber Optic Point Sensing focuses on specific locations, providing precise measurements of parameters such as temperature and strain at designated points. Both types leverage the unique properties of FBGs for effective and reliable sensing applications.</p></p>
