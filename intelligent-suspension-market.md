<p><p>The intelligent suspension market is divided into two primary types: automatic suspension and semi-automatic suspension. Automatic suspension systems dynamically adjust damping and ride height in real-time based on road conditions and vehicle dynamics, providing optimal comfort and handling without driver intervention. In contrast, semi-automatic suspension systems allow for some manual adjustments by the driver while still offering automated features, balancing user control with comfort. Both types aim to enhance vehicle performance, comfort, and safety, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
