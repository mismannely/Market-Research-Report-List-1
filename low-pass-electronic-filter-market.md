<p><ul><li>Passive Electronic Filter</li><li>Active Electronic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-pass electronic filter market can be categorized into two main types: passive and active electronic filters. Passive filters use resistors, capacitors, and inductors to allow low-frequency signals to pass while attenuating higher frequencies, relying on the inherent properties of these components without requiring external power. In contrast, active filters utilize operational amplifiers and active components, enabling improved performance, higher efficiency, and greater design flexibility. Both types serve crucial roles in applications such as audio processing, telecommunications, and signal conditioning.</p></p>
