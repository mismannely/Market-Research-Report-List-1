Within the MDO market, Light Diesel Oil (LDO) is characterized by lower viscosity and is suitable for smaller vessels and lighter operations. Heavy-Duty Diesel Oil (HDDO), on the other hand, has higher viscosity, providing enhanced lubrication and durability for larger marine vessels operating under intense conditions. Both types cater to different operational requirements in the maritime industry.</p></p>
