Nanoparticle coatings leverage advanced materials for targeted drug delivery and enhanced anti-microbial properties. Other coatings may include polymers, ceramics, or bioactive glasses, each designed to address specific clinical needs and improve the longevity and performance of medical implants.</p></p>
