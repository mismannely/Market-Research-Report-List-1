<p><p>The mobile phone battery market comprises several key types, including Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd), Nickel-Hydrogen (NiH), and Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) batteries. NiCd batteries are known for their durability but suffer from memory effect and lower energy density. NiH batteries offer improved energy capacity and reduced environmental impact, making them suitable for specific applications. However, Lithium-Ion batteries dominate the market due to their high energy density, lightweight design, and longer lifespan, making them the preferred choice for modern smartphones and portable devices.</p></p>
