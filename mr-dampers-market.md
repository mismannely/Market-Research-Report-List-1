<p><p>Magnetorheological (MR) dampers are advanced devices used in various applications like automotive and civil engineering to enhance performance and safety by controlling vibrations. The market is categorized into active and semi-active dampers. Active dampers adjust their damping force based on real-time input, providing optimal performance but requiring an external power source. Semi-active dampers, on the other hand, use a passive system influenced by electromagnetic fields, allowing for adjustments without complete reliance on power, offering a balance of efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
