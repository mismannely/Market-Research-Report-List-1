<p><strong>The Plastic Extrusion Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Screw</li><li>Twin-Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic extrusion machinery is categorized mainly into single-screw and twin-screw types, each serving specific applications. Single-screw extruders are simpler and cost-effective, suited for processing a variety of thermoplastics. They operate by pushing material through a heated barrel using a single screw. In contrast, twin-screw extruders utilize two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, compounding, and processing of materials. This design enhances flexibility and efficiency, making it ideal for advanced applications, including polymers and specialized compounds.</p></p>
