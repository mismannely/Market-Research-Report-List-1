<p><strong>The PTFE Architectural Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE Fabric</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE architectural fabrics market comprises two primary types: porous and non-porous PTFE fabrics. Porous PTFE fabric allows air and moisture to pass through, making it suitable for applications requiring breathability and ventilation. In contrast, non-porous PTFE fabric serves as a barrier, offering superior waterproofing and UV resistance, ideal for shelters and canopies. Both types are valued for their durability, weather resistance, and aesthetic versatility, making them popular choices in modern architectural designs.</p></p>
