<p><p>The Radio Frequency (RF) Relays market can be categorized into two primary types: DC type and AC type. DC RF relays are designed to operate with direct current, providing quick switching capabilities for low-voltage applications, often favored in telecommunications and control systems. Conversely, AC RF relays operate with alternating current, suitable for higher voltage applications and widely used in power management and industrial settings. Both types play critical roles in enabling efficient wireless communication and signal routing in various electronic devices.</p></p>
