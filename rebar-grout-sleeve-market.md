<p><p>The rebar grout sleeve market is segmented into full-grout sleeves and half-grout sleeves. Full-grout sleeves are designed to allow complete encapsulation of rebar, providing maximum bond strength and protection against environmental factors. In contrast, half-grout sleeves offer partial encapsulation, balancing ease of installation and cost-effectiveness while still providing reasonable protection. These products cater to different construction needs, with full-grout sleeves generally preferred for high-strength applications, while half-grout sleeves are utilized in situations where flexibility and budget considerations are prioritized.</p></p>
