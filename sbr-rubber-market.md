<p><strong>The SBR Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion-SBR (E-SBR)</li><li>Solution-SBR (S-SBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SBR rubber market comprises two main types: Emulsion-SBR (E-SBR) and Solution-SBR (S-SBR). E-SBR is produced through emulsion polymerization, resulting in a rubber with excellent aging resistance and suitable for tire manufacturing. In contrast, S-SBR is created using solution polymerization, offering superior wet traction and processing benefits, making it ideal for high-performance tires. Both types cater to various applications, including automotive, industrial, and consumer goods, driven by demand for enhanced performance and sustainability in rubber products.</p></p>
