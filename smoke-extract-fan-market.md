Axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis, offering compact designs ideal for smaller spaces with lower resistance. The "Others" category encompasses specialized fans, including hybrid models and customized solutions tailored for unique smoke control scenarios, enhancing safety in various environments.</p></p>
