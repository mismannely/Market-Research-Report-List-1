<p><p>The structural steel fabrication market encompasses various processes used to shape and assemble steel components. Metal welding involves joining materials through heat, while metal forming shapes steel using techniques such as bending or pressing. Metal cutting entails removing excess material to create desired shapes, and metal shearing involves cutting sheets to size. Metal stamping is used for creating complex shapes via dies, while metal rolling processes reshape steel into specific profiles. Other methods include machining and finishing techniques, enhancing the versatility of the fabrication process.</p></p>
