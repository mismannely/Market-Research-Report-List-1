<p><p>The Syphilis Kits market is divided into two main types: Non-Treponemal tests and Treponemal tests. Non-Treponemal tests detect antibodies produced during the syphilis infection, providing initial screening results, but may yield false positives. Treponemal tests, on the other hand, confirm infection by detecting antibodies specific to the Treponema pallidum bacterium, ensuring a more accurate diagnosis. Together, these test types enhance syphilis detection and management, playing a crucial role in public health strategies for controlling the disease.</p></p>
